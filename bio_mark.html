Mark Yee: keyboards, saxophone, backing vocals

  

Mark Yee has been playing the piano for a long, long, time. He studied saxophone under Dante Perfumo, playing transcriptions of classical violin solos. Highlighting his eclectic musical tastes and influences, his band experience includes hosting a weekly blues jam with the Ted Reyes Group, co-founding 70s cover band Serious Cheese, and a short stint attempting contemporary jazz with Russell Faraldi. Good fortune and a Craig's List posting brought Mark into contact with Slippery People founder, Bill Donte, and the rest is history.
